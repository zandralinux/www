About Zandra

Zandra is an independent musl-based Linux distribution based on the principles of simplicity and efficiency. It is a fork of morpheus linux. It uses Plan 9 mkfiles to build packages, with both a ports tree and a binary package manager. All binaries are statically linked. Every aspect of the system tries to conform to the Unix philosophy of "do one thing and do it well".
